About

The IT Academy (ITA) is a 2-year, dual enrollment program that is exclusive to Dublin City Schools. The course is designed for those who have a strong interest int IT and Computer Science. Here at ITA, your kids will learn many materials and skills that will be useful throughtout their lifetime. One of the many great things of this academy is that you will learn HTML, JavaScript, SQl, Python, CSS, and Visual basic; many languages that thousands of companies use. With this program you will do the course work that Columbus State requires and you will work on your own personal project involoving either a Raspberry Pi or an Arduino. The ITA is held at Dublin Scioto High School and is only available for Juniors and Seniors at any of the three Dublin high schools. Click Here for more information

What is Dual enrollment?

Dual Enrollment means you will earn high shcool credit as well as college credit through Columbus State. Columbus State and Tolles are partners and creators of the ITA which is why you will be doing Columbus State work and high school work. By the end of the two years you will have earned 16 college credit hours at Columbus State. Fortunaility, those credit hours will transfer to any in state college that you choose to go to.

Requirements

There are a few requirements you must pass before you can start the application process. Those requirements are:

  • You must be a Junior
  • Have three free periods in your day
  • Have a 3.5 Cumulative GPA or higher

If you pass those requirements then you can proceed to the application process. If you are not sure if you have enough time in your day for the IT Academy you can email your counselor by clicking here(add link to staff look up) and look up your counselor to email him or her.
